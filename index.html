ABOUT

The influence of digital has transcended all industries and economies. The incredible rise of digital technologies and the success of digital native companies have accelerated intrest and shift towards digital transformation of business. Digital transformation is delivering a success strategy by enabling innovative customer engagement, effcient operations and reduced costs.

For the business leaders, digital execution poses a challenge of uncovering a clear strategy of transcending from where the business stands to where it intends to move; the direction more important than the destination.

The Digital NEXT will connect leaders & practitioners working towards a digital transformation strategy on a common agenda that will focus on three core pillars of transformation; people, technology & process. The immersive format is designed to drive collaboration between technology and business leaders to explore the scope and benefits of digitising the horizontal and vertical value chains.

Join 200+ of India's top business leaders from the Digital Native ecosystem on a day of insightful sessions & inovative solutions sure to inspire and enhance your digital strategy!
